Letter From The Secretary General

Welcome to STEM Model Arab League in its First Edition

Dear Participants and faculty advisors,

On behalf of our secretariat and volunteers, it is my honor to invite you all to the first conference of its kind in STEM Schools. STEM schools are considered one of the most elite schools in the country and have been the origin for many renowned public speakers, creative thinkers and of course, delegates . Taking place in Cairo, STEM MAL’s delegates will debate, research and pass resolutions that seek to resolve some of the Arab countries' most difficult challenges. Throughout this process, students will gain public speaking skills, debating skills and will learn about the history of the Arab world through our rigorous agenda.

Any STEM high scholar is invited to apply and enjoy this great experience as a delegate. Unfortunately, due to the current circumstances, the exact dates for the conference and the registration are not known and are very subjectable for change. More information will be released on our website and social media once things are clearer.

Our innovative approach this year will be through our two unique councils, Historic Events Council and Joint Defense Council. Thus, adding exciting and challenging committees while endeavoring new approaches to Model Arab League.

Browse through our website to learn more about STEM MAL, and please contact us if you have any questions.

I look forward to welcoming you in person on the conference day.


Regards,

Farid Wahba

Secretary-General
